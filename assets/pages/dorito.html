Dorito Drills

Training Tips:

Training for the Dorito Side in speedball requires a combination of strategic understanding, agility, and effective communication with teammates. Here are some training tips to enhance performance on the Dorito Side:

  • Master the Breakout:
    • Practice various breakout strategies to quickly and safely reach your initial Dorito bunker. Work on your sprinting technique, sliding into the bunker, and minimizing exposure to opponents.
  • Field Awareness:
    • Develop a keen awareness of the Dorito Side layout, including bunkers, lanes, and opponent positions. This knowledge is crucial for making effective moves and avoiding enemy fire.
  • Dorito Bunker Transition:
    • Practice transitioning between Dorito bunkers smoothly. Learn when to move and choose the optimal path to advance down the Dorito Side while maintaining cover.
