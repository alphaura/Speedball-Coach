Diving Technique

  • Go through the videos first for a better understanding of the technique and drill
  • 2 markers/cones (anything would do)
  • Level 1: Take 5 steps back from marker, no equipment, focus on dive form and then power
  • 5 reps on each side x 3 sets
  • Level 1 Progression: Place a second marker on the first 5 steps, and take 5 more steps back, run low and perform the dive
  • 5 reps on each side x 2 sets
  • Level 2: Repeat level 1 wearing full gear
  • 5 reps on each side x 3 sets
  • Level 2 Progression: Repeat level 1 progression wearing full gear
  • 5 reps on each side x 2 sets

Crawling Technique

  • Medium/Long line of obstacles
  • Video 1 & 2, different crawling styles
  • Video 3, some information to when and how they are used
  • 5 reps on each side x3 sets on any style crawl you want
  • 2 minute rest between sets
  • Tip: Look at pro players to understand the movement in real-time

Snake Snapshooting

  • Obstacle + 1 target
  • Start close and progress further as you go along
  • 1 shot per snap
  • 10 shots on each side x3 sets
  • 1 minute rest between sets
  • Tip: Aim to increase the amount of hits made with each set

Cooldown

  • Hold each stretch for 30s each side
  • Long Standing Calf Stretch
  • Short Standing Calf Stretch
  • Standing adductor stretch
  • 90/90 hamstring stretch
  • Supine bicep femoris stretch
  • Kneeling hip flexor stretch
  • Supine figure 4
