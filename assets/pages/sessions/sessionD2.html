Dorito Gunfighting Techniques

Intermediate

Overview

Enhance your gunfighting skills specifically for the Dorito side. Focus on snap shooting, laning, and engaging opponents from various Dorito bunkers. Whilst some drills are not directly targeted for the Dorito side, the knowledge is still useful.

  • Jogging x5 field rounds + gun + mask
  • Hold each stretch for 2s
  • Active standing adductors (x5 each side)
  • Active 90/90 hamstring stretch (x5 each side)
  • Active supine bicep femoris stretch (x5 each side)

Snapshooting Drill

  • Obstacle + target
  • Target: 9m distance
  • 5 reps each side x3 sets
  • 1 minute rest between sets
  • Tip: Focus on movement and accuracy first. Once you're confident, go ahead with increased speed and time

Laning Drill

  • Starting point + target
  • Target placed on the corners of the field
  • 3 reps each side x3 sets
  • 1 minute rest between sets
  • Tip: Don't move close to the bunker
  • Tip: Front foot pointing where you're shooting
  • Tip: Walk the field and understand the potential run of the opponents
  • Bonus: Have a teammate be the runner and switch

Positional Gunfighting

  • Obstacle + 1/2 targets
  • Targets 15m distance from obstacle
  • 4 confirmed shots per rep: Top-right, Bottom-right, Top-left, Bottom-left
  • 5 reps x3 sets
  • 1 minute rest between sets

Cooldown

  • Hold each stretch for 30s each side
  • Long Standing Calf Stretch
  • Short Standing Calf Stretch
  • Standing adductor stretch
  • 90/90 hamstring stretch
  • Supine bicep femoris stretch
  • Kneeling hip flexor stretch
  • Supine figure 4
