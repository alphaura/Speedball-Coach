Shooting Warm-up

  • 1 target + 3 markers
  • 10m distance from first marker and 5m distance from the other two
  • 10 shots then move to next spot
  • 30 shots in total x2 sets

Run'n'Gun

  • 2/3 targets + 2 markers
  • 9m distance run
  • 15m distance from targets
  • Clear the loader and rest for 30s
  • Perform drill for 3 sets
  • Aim: Hit each target with 1-3 shots

Zig-Zag Run'n'Gun to snap

  • 2/3 targets + 4 bunkers/ obstacles
  • Zig-zag from bunker to bunker
  • Snapshoot outside a few times then run'n'gun to the next bunker
  • 5 reps x3 sets
  • Rest 30s/rep & 1 minute/set

C-Block Shooting Drill

  • 3 targets with 4 sections
  • Section 1: Breakout run'n'gun to the corner
  • Section 2: Snapshoot right-handed from the corner leading to a run'n'dive
  • Section 3: Snapshoot left-handed from last bunker
  • Section 4: Run'n'gun from last bunker to a target downfield
  • Repeat each section for 3x, rest only 1 minute between each section
  • Do all sections altogether 3x, rest for 1 minute between each run

Cooldown

  • Hold each stretch for 30s each side
  • Long Standing Calf Stretch
  • Short Standing Calf Stretch
  • Standing adductor stretch
  • 90/90 hamstring stretch
  • Supine bicep femoris stretch
  • Kneeling hip flexor stretch
  • Supine figure 4
