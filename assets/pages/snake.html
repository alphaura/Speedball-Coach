Snake Drills

Training Tips:

Training for the Snake Side in speedball demands a blend of strategic insight, agility, and adept communication with teammates. Here are some training tips to enhance performance on the Dorito Side:

  • Quick Breakouts and Efficient Movement:
    • Prioritize developing rapid breakout techniques to swiftly reach the Snake bunker at the beginning of each match. Focus on efficient movements, including sprinting, sliding, and diving, to secure a strategic position early on.
  • Effective Snapshooting and Dynamic Movements:
    • Hone your snapshooting skills from various Snake bunkers, emphasizing quick and accurate shots while minimizing exposure. Incorporate dynamic movements such as sliding, crawling, and diving to make yourself a challenging target and maintain control of the Snake side.
  • Clear and Precise Communication:
    • Establish clear and precise communication with teammates on the Snake side. Effective communication is paramount for coordinating movements, sharing vital information, and executing strategic plays. Stay connected with your team to enhance overall performance and adapt to changing game dynamics.
